MEET THE

FRATERNITY

#1

CODY

THE PRESIDENT

The president of the frat, Cody is a quarterback and captain of the college football team. Cody originally from Los Angeles gained his place into college through a scholarship for his outstanding football talent. Cody's other hobbies involve cute girls and excessive drinking.

#2

JACKSON

THE VICE PRESIDENT

Jackson is vice president of the frat, originally from Miami Florida, like Cody he also gained a scholarship. When at home Jackson resides on Muscle beach, whilst at college you will mostly find him in the gym or canteen. The Miamians other hobbies include listening to Vanilla Ice and selling illegal merchandise from his basement.

#3

LOGAN

The Developer

The smartest and most desired of the turtles. Logan is already set up for life with a paid internship at his dad successful Law firm after college. Logan is one to never miss the party, and although linked to a law firm is known specializing in the sales of Nitrous Oxide and cannabis.

#4

JAYCE

The Developer

Jayce originates from Las Vegas, Nevada. Much like his hometown Jayce is a Turtle that rarely sleeps and much prefers to party. Unlike his fellow frat mates, Jayce is failing his studies, skipping class and spending Daddys money on underage gambling and liquor.
